Tashin Hossain

Lecturer || NLP Researcher

Hello! I am Tashin Hossain. I am a recent graduate from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at University of Chittagong, Bangladesh and currently employed as a Lecturer in the Department of of Computer Science and Engineering of Premier University, Chittagong, Bangladesh.

My primary research lies in Natural Language Processing, but it also includes Large Language Model, Computational Social Science, Multimodal Information Processing, and Information Retrieval.

I enjoy outdoor activities (e.g. travelling) and watching animation movies.
